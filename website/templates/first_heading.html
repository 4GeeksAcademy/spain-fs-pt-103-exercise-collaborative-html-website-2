Rauw Alejandro

Rauw Alejandro, the Latin GRAMMY® award-winning and GRAMMY®-nominated Puerto Rican performer has transcended borders with his dynamic presence and global musical influence since bursting onto the scene in 2017. His debut album Afrodisíaco, released in November 2020, swiftly earned Gold certification in the USA and Colombia, setting the stage for his meteoric rise. With the release of Vice Versa in June 2021, Rauw Alejandro cemented his position as a global music icon. The album soared to #1 on Billboard’s Top Latin Album chart, #3 on Spotify’s Top Debut Album chart, and #1 on Apple Music’s Latin Album charts, accumulating over 3.6 billion streams. Praised by esteemed media outlets like The New York Times, Rolling Stone, NPR, and Bill- board, Rauw’s performance prowess reached unparalleled heights.
